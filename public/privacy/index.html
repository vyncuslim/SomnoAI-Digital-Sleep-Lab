Privacy Policy

Somno Digital Sleep Lab • v2026.01.28

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

Somno Lab’s use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

1. Data Analytics (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymized usage telemetry. This includes:

  • Page views and navigation patterns.
  • Session duration and frequency.
  • Technical specifications of the access node (browser type, OS).

This data is used exclusively to optimize laboratory performance and infrastructure stability.

2. AI Processing (Google Gemini)

Somno Lab utilizes Advanced AI Models (Google Gemini) to perform deep synthesis of biological telemetry. Your de-identified metrics are transmitted to neural processing nodes to generate personalized sleep insights and optimization protocols.

3. Data Storage Location

Somno Lab utilizes a Secure Edge Architecture.

  • Local Processing: Your raw physiological metrics (heart rate, motion, etc.) are stored exclusively in your browser's sessionStorage.
  • Zero Backend Persistence: Sensitive biometric data is never transmitted to or stored on our permanent cloud databases.

4. Data Deletion & User Rights

You maintain absolute sovereignty over your laboratory data:

  • Immediate Purge: All synchronized physiological data is permanently deleted from your browser session upon Logout or closing the tab.
  • Account Erasure: You may request the deletion of your laboratory identity registry at any time by contacting the administrator.
  • Revocation: Access permissions to Google Health data can be revoked via your Google Account Security Settings.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, lease, or share your personal health data with third parties for commercial or advertising purposes. All data processing is strictly for personal health optimization within the Somno Lab environment.
