Google API Limited Use Disclosure

SomnoAI's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Service User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

1. 数据访问与收集 / Data Collection

当您授权连接 Google Fit 时，本应用会访问您的睡眠分段、心率及活动能量数据。这些数据仅用于应用内的数字化睡眠实验分析。

We access your health data (sleep segments, heart rate, and activity) via Google APIs only with your explicit consent for the purpose of personal health visualization and AI analysis.

2. 数据存储声明 / Data Storage

零服务器存储：本应用不设后端服务器。所有数据仅存储在您浏览器的本地会话（sessionStorage）中，一旦关闭页面或退出登录，数据将立即被永久抹除。我们不保留任何用户生理数据的持久副本。
