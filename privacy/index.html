Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 22, 2024

1. Overview

SomnoAI Digital Sleep Lab ("the App") is a digital sleep experiment platform. We are committed to protecting your health data through a "Client-Side Only" architecture. We do not store your raw health data on any cloud servers.

2. Google API Limited Use Disclosure

SomnoAI's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Service User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

  • Your Google Fit data is used only to provide sleep visualizations and AI-generated insights within your browser.
  • We do not share your health data with any third-party advertisers or data brokers.
  • We do not use your Google Fit data to train or improve AI/ML models.

3. Data Collection (Google Fit)

Upon your explicit authorization, we access the following data via Google Fit API:

  • Sleep Data (fitness.sleep.read): To visualize your sleep stages (Deep, REM, Light).
  • Heart Rate (fitness.heart_rate.read): To analyze your resting heart rate during sleep.
  • Activity (fitness.activity.read): To calculate energy expenditure (calories) for metabolic insights.

4. Data Storage & Deletion

All data is stored in your browser's Session Storage. This data is transient and is automatically deleted when you close your browser tab or click the "Logout" button in the App.

5. AI Processing

The App uses Google Gemini API to analyze your metrics. During this process, data is anonymized. We do not transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) like your name or email to the AI engine.
