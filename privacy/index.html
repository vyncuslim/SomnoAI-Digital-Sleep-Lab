Last Updated: March 24, 2024

Google API Limited Use Disclosure

SomnoAI's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Service User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

1. Overview

SomnoAI Digital Sleep Lab ("the App") respects your privacy. This policy describes how we handle health data received via Google Fit APIs.

2. Data Collection & Use

We access the following data from your Google Fit account only after you grant permission:

  • Sleep Data: To visualize sleep stages and calculate sleep quality scores.
  • Heart Rate: To analyze recovery and resting heart rate patterns.
  • Activity Data: To correlate energy expenditure with sleep results.

We do not share health data with third-party advertisers, brokers, or use it for AI model training.

3. Local Data Processing

All processing happens locally in your web browser. Health data is stored in Session Storage and is wiped automatically when you log out or close the browser tab. We do not maintain any external database of your health information.
