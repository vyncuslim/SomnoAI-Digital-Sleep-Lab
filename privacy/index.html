Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 23, 2024

1. Introduction

SomnoAI Digital Sleep Lab ("the App") is a wellness platform focused on digital sleep analysis. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the health data you share with us through Google Fit.

Google API Limited Use Disclosure

SomnoAI's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Service User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

• We only use Google Fit data to provide personalized sleep insights and visualizations.

• No Advertising: Your health data is never shared with third-party advertisers or data brokers.

• No AI Training: We do not use your personal health data to train, retrain, or improve any machine learning or AI models.

2. Data Collection (Google Fit)

Upon your explicit consent, we request access to:

  • Sleep Data (fitness.sleep.read): To analyze sleep duration and cycles.
  • Heart Rate (fitness.heart_rate.read): To calculate resting heart rate during sleep.
  • Activity (fitness.activity.read): To estimate calories burned and activity levels.

3. Data Storage & Deletion

We believe in Data Sovereignty. Your data is processed locally in your browser and stored in Session Storage. We do not maintain any persistent databases on our servers.

How to delete your data: Simply click the "Logout" button in the App or clear your browser's site data. You can also revoke our access at any time via your Google Account Security Settings.
