SomnoAI Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 4, 2026

1. Data Access

SomnoAI accesses the following Google user data with your explicit authorization: sleep segments, heart rate, and activity energy. This data is used solely for digital sleep analysis and personalized AI insights within the application.

2. Data Usage

All data is processed exclusively to analyze your individual sleep patterns and generate visual reports. Data will not be used for advertising, marketing, or sold to third parties. Data processing occurs only within your local browser session.

3. Data Storage

SomnoAI does not maintain backend servers for data storage. All data is saved only in the browser's local session (sessionStorage) and is immediately deleted upon closing the page or logging out.

4. Your Rights

You can revoke access authorization for SomnoAI at any time in your Google account settings. Contact the developer at: ongyuze1401@gmail.com
