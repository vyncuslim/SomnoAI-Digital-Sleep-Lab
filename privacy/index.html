Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 23, 2024

1. Overview

SomnoAI Digital Sleep Lab ("the App") respects user privacy. This policy explains how we process health data obtained through Google Fit APIs.

Google API Limited Use Disclosure

SomnoAI's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Service User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

2. Data Collection & Use

We access the following categories of data only after your explicit consent:

  • Sleep Data: Used to visualize sleep stages and calculate sleep quality scores.
  • Heart Rate: Used to analyze recovery and resting heart rate patterns.
  • Activity Data: Correlated with sleep duration to provide health insights.

We do not share your health data with third-party advertisers or use it for AI model training.

3. Local Storage & Security

All health data processing occurs locally in your browser session. Data is stored in Session Storage and is automatically erased when you log out or close the browser tab. We do not maintain any persistent external databases.
