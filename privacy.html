Somno Digital Sleep Lab operates on a Zero-Backend Localized Architecture. We prioritize user sovereignty over biometric data. This document details the specific telemetry accessed via Google Fit APIs and our commitment to the Google Limited Use policy.

Google API Limited Use Disclosure

Somno's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Limited Use Commitment: We do not use your Google Fit data for advertising, profiling, or sharing with any third-party data brokers. Your health data is used strictly to provide sleep insights within this application.

1. Telemetry Schema (Data Accessed)

To synthesize your "Sleep Architecture Map," the lab requests read-only access to the following Google Fit datasets:

  • DATASET_01
    Sleep Segments (com.google.sleep.segment)

    Used to identify Deep, REM, and Light sleep stages. This forms the baseline for our AI-driven Sleep Architecture Analysis.

  • DATASET_02
    Heart Rate Telemetry (com.google.heart_rate.bpm)

    Used to calculate Resting Heart Rate (RHR) and heart rate variability during sleep, essential for assessing physiological recovery levels.

  • DATASET_03
    Metabolic Flow (com.google.calories.expended)

    Used to correlate energy expenditure with sleep efficiency, providing a multi-dimensional view of your physiological state.

2. Processing Protocol (Usage)

The accessed telemetry is processed as follows:

  • Sleep Architecture Analysis: We calculate the ratio of sleep stages to generate a subjective "Sleep Score."
  • Physiological Projections: Our engine uses localized AI models to project potential cognitive and physical recovery impacts for the following day.
  • Neural Synthesis: Select metrics are sent as an encrypted transient payload to the Gemini API to generate personalized health suggestions. No data is stored during this process.

3. Security & Persistence (Zero-Backend)

Somno is engineered with a Privacy-First Architecture.

Transit Security

Data is synchronized directly from Google's servers to your browser's RAM via SSL/TLS. No Somno-owned servers ever touch your biometric data.

Storage Logic

Sensitive metrics are held in sessionStorage. Unlike cookies or local storage, this data is automatically wiped when you close the browser tab.
