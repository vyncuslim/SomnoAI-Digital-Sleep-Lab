Somno Digital Sleep Lab (sleepsomno.com) operates on a Zero-Backend Encryption Architecture. This document outlines how our neural synthesis engine handles biometric signals synchronized via Google Fit APIs.

Google API Limited Use Disclosure

Somno's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We do not share your health data with third-party researchers, marketers, or advertisers.

1. Signal Access (Telemetry)

  • 0x01 Sleep Architecture: Reading segments (Deep, REM, Light) to synthesize your sleep score.
  • 0x02 Cardiac Telemetry: Accessing heart rate (BPM) to calibrate physiological recovery metrics.
  • 0x03 Metabolic Flow: Reading energy expenditure (Calories) for multi-dimensional architecture mapping.

2. Storage Protocol (Zero-Persistence)

Our platform is engineered for Total Privacy. Biometric data is synchronized directly to your browser's ephemeral sessionStorage.

Security Verification:

  • No cloud databases / No centralized storage.
  • Automated session purge upon tab closure or manual logout.
  • Encrypted local handshakes only.

3. Information Control

You maintain absolute sovereignty over your telemetry streams. You can terminate the lab link at any time by clearing your browser cache or revoking access via your Google Security Settings.
