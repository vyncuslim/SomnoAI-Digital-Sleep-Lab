Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 24, 2024

1. Introduction

SomnoAI Digital Sleep Lab ("the App") is a wellness tool designed to help you understand your sleep patterns. This Privacy Policy explains how we process data accessed via Google Fit APIs.

Google API Limited Use Disclosure

SomnoAI's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Service User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

2. Data Collection & Usage

We only access data categories you explicitly authorize via the Google OAuth consent screen:

  • Sleep Sessions: To visualize your sleep stages (Deep, REM, Light, Awake).
  • Heart Rate: To calculate resting heart rate and analyze recovery.
  • Activity/Calories: To correlate physical activity with sleep quality.

We do NOT sell your data, use it for advertising, or use your health data for training artificial intelligence models.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data processing happens locally in your browser. We use Session Storage to temporarily store metrics while you use the app. This data is automatically purged when you log out or close your browser tab. We do not store your health data on any external servers.
